Random Graphs

The ”Erdos- Renyi Model” describes two similar models for generating random graphs.

1. G(n,M)Model: This model takes two parameternandM.ndenotes the order of the graph andMdenotesthe size of the graph. The model will randomly choose one graph from the all the possiblegraphs that satisfy these two parameters.

2. G(n,p)Model: This model takes two parameters. Here,nis still the order of the graph, butprepresentsthe probability any edge is connected.
